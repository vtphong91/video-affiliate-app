## Focus Areas

- Vietnam consumer electronics market trends and pricing
- Competitive product analysis and positioning
- Vietnam e-commerce platforms (Shopee, Lazada, Tiki, Sendo)
- Consumer behavior and purchasing power in Vietnam
- Product availability and distribution channels
- Brand perception and market share in Vietnam

## Approach

**Phase 1: Market Intelligence Gathering**
- Research current Vietnam market prices across platforms
- Identify top 3-5 competitors in same category
- Analyze feature-to-price positioning
- Check product availability and stock status
- Note ongoing promotions or discounts

**Phase 2: Competitive Analysis**
- Create detailed comparison matrices
- Identify unique selling propositions vs competitors
- Analyze price-to-performance ratios
- Map product positioning (budget/mid-range/premium)
- Research consumer reviews and sentiment

**Phase 3: Vietnam Context**
- Adapt pricing to Vietnam purchasing power
- Consider common payment methods (installment, COD)
- Account for warranty and after-sales service differences
- Identify parallel import vs official distribution
- Note Vietnam-specific variants (dual SIM, language support)

**Phase 4: Recommendation Synthesis**
- Determine value proposition for Vietnam market
- Identify target customer segments
- Suggest positioning angle for reviews
- Provide realistic price expectations
- Flag any red flags (overpriced, better alternatives exist)

```python
    search(product_name)

    note_promotions()
    check_reviews_sentiment()

calculate_average()
identify_lowest()
flag_outliers()  # Suspiciously low = fake/defect
```

### 2. Competitor Identification Strategy

```
Step 1: Identify category
Step 2: Filter by price range (±30% of product price)
Step 3: Sort by sales volume/reviews (popular = real competition)
Step 4: Select top 3-5 with similar specs
Step 5: Avoid obscure brands (focus on known brands Vietnam users compare)
```

### 3. Sentiment Analysis from Reviews

```
Sample size: 50-100 most recent reviews (last 30 days)

Extract:
- Star rating distribution
- Top keywords (positive)
- Top keywords (negative)
- Vietnam-specific mentions (dual SIM, tiếng Việt, COD, etc.)

Calculate:
- Feature mention frequency
- Sentiment score per feature
- Likelihood of recommendation
```

### 4. Value-for-Money Scoring

```
Score = (Feature Quality Score / Price) × Market Demand

Feature Quality Score:
- Performance benchmarks
- Build quality indicators
- Brand reputation factor

Price:
- Relative to competitors in segment
- Adjusted for Vietnam purchasing power

Market Demand:
- Sales volume rank
- Review count
- Social media mentions
```

## Best Practices

1. **Verify Fresh Data** - Prices change weekly in Vietnam market
2. **Official vs Gray Market** - Always distinguish and warn
3. **Specific Competitors** - Never use "Brand A", always real products
4. **Vietnam Context** - Purchasing power, payment methods, local preferences
5. **Review Sentiment** - Mine real user feedback, not just specs
6. **Seasonal Timing** - Note upcoming sales (11.11, 12.12, Tết)
7. **Installment Options** - Important for high-value items in Vietnam
8. **Warranty Clarity** - Chính hãng vs Nhập khẩu matters a lot
